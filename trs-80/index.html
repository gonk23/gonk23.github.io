TRS-80

This TRS-80 Model III was originally an Australian delivery cassette-based 16KB level II machine (cat 269-1062), but was gradually upgraded over the years to have 48KB RAM, two SSDD (180KB) 5.25" floppy drives, RS232 serial port, and Radio Shack high resolution graphics board.
